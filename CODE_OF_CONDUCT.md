# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@contextlinter.ai**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1, available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
